WWF

The World Wide Fund for Nature

WWF (The World Wide Fund for Nature) is the world's leading conservation charity. They work to conserve natural resources as well as natural habitats and environments

They work in nearly 100 countries and have ,globally, 5 million supporters

Their work is focused around 6 ambitious goals:

  • Climate: Creating a climate-resilient and zero-carbon world
  • Food: Double net food availability
  • Forests: Conserve the World's most important forests
  • Freshwater: Secure water for people and nature
  • Oceans: Safeguard healthy oceans and marine livelihoods
  • Wildlife: Protects the World's most important species

    • Overall, WWF try to represent and exspress 4 major values: Courage, Integrity, Respect and Collaboration

    Their major goals is to halt nature loss and put it on the path to recovery by 2030
